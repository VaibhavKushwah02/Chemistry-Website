   Aluminium(or aluminium; see difeerent endings) is a chemical element in the bororn group with the symbol Al and atomic number 13. It is a silvery-white, soft, nonmagnetic, ductile metal. Aluminium is the third most abundant element(after oxygen and silicon), and the most abundant metal, in the Earth's crust.
