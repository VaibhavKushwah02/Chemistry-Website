   Oxygen is the chemical element with the symbol O and atomic number 8. It is a member of the chalcogen group in the periodic table and highly reactive nonmetal and an oxidizing agent that readily forms compounds(notably oxides) with most elements. By mass, oxygen is the third-most abundant element in the universe, after hydrogen and helium.
