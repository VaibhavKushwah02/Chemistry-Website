   Yttrium is a chemical element with symbol Y and atomic number 39.It is a silvery-metallic transition metal chemically similar to the lanthanides and its has often been classificied as a "rare earth element". Yttrium is almost always found combined with the lanthanides in rare earth minerals and is never found in nature as a free element.
